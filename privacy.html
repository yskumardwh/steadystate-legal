Privacy Policy

SteadyState · Last updated June 2026

SteadyState is built on the principle that your health data belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what we collect, where it stays, and what we do with it.

What data SteadyState collects

  • Accelerometer data — a 10-second reading taken during each scan. Processed on-device into a stability score. The raw sensor data is never stored or transmitted.
  • Reaction time — tap timestamps from each trial. Stored locally as a computed median score.
  • Mood and tags — the mood and tag you select before each scan (e.g. "Coffee", "Post-Workout").
  • Scan history — date, time, scores, mood, and tag for each completed scan.
  • Your name — optional, entered in Settings. Used only for personalised greetings within the app.
  • Wake time preference — used to schedule morning reminders at the right time.

HealthKit data

With your permission, SteadyState reads the following from Apple Health:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Resting Heart Rate
  • Sleep Analysis (total, deep, REM)
  • Respiratory Rate
  • Stand Hours

This data is read-only — SteadyState never writes to Apple Health. HealthKit data is used solely to generate your Morning Brief and Day Ahead summaries within the app. It is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Where your data is stored

All scan data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced to your private CloudKit container — a private database only you can access. Apple's CloudKit infrastructure handles encryption in transit and at rest. SteadyState has no access to your iCloud data.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or analytics platforms.
  • We do not have a backend server that receives your health or scan data.
  • We do not use tracking SDKs or third-party analytics libraries.
  • We do not have access to your Apple Health data — it stays on your device and in your private iCloud.

Notifications

SteadyState may send local push notifications (morning reminders, paired scan reminders, weekly digests). These are scheduled entirely on-device and do not involve any external server. You can disable all notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications or in iOS Settings → Notifications → SteadyState.

Deleting your data

You can delete all your scan and reaction time data at any time from Settings → Data → Delete All Data. This removes data from your device and from iCloud. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Children

SteadyState is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
